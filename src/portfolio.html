In a group of five, I participated in the UI redesign of Southern California Edison's mobile app. In the redesign process, my group and I went through competitive analysis, user research, usability evaluation, redesign, and heuristic evaluation. Before redesign, we evaluated the app's UI from different perspectives and gathered feedback from real users using methods like interview and survey. In the redesign process, we prioritized the most urgent and important problems, and tried to resolve them one by one. After the redesign is done, we evaluated our redesign to better understand it.
